The Contemporary

The Contemporary is a nomadic, non-collecting art museum in Baltimore, Maryland. We are committed to redefining the concept of a museum to be open, inclusive, and available to all. We believe that artists are essential to our world and play a unique role within society: to take risks, see things differently, and dismantle social boundaries. We move throughout the city connecting artists, partners, and communities, to create ambitious public programming that is responsive, challenging, and educational. Our work is inspired by three guiding principles: artists matter, collaboration is key, and audience is everywhere.
